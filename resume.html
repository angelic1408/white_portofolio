Resume

Hello, my name is Angelia! But you can call me An. I'm a designer based in Japan. My passion is to turn a complex problem into a beautiful, intuitive solutions by understanding users' need and business value.

I've been working on crafting digital experience for the past 3 years. I worked with Rakuten and Sony and I was entrusted to design some fundamental service such as Rakuten Pay LITE online payment and Sony Global Websites. My design process involves in-depth user research as well as data analytics. I believe that qualitative and quantitative research are equally important in crafting a better user experience.

If I'm not designing, I'm usually travelling, taking photos, and writing my experience on my blog. I do sports too. I'm really forward talking to you.
